# Week 2: Resume, Personal Brand & Application Mastery

This workshop focuses on helping students build a strong, ATS-friendly resume and a clear personal brand—even with little or no experience—and apply strategically to internships and jobs.

## What This Covers
- Resume building from scratch using projects and skills
- Personal branding and defining a clear career narrative
- Resume tailoring for different roles
- Writing effective cover letters
- Answering key application questions like “Why us?” and “Why this role?”

## Outcome
By the end of this week, students will have:
- A polished, role-ready resume
- A consistent personal brand across resume and LinkedIn
- A clear strategy to apply smarter, not harder
- Strong answers for common application questions
