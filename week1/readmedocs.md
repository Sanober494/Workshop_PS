# LinkedIn Essentials Workshop for College Students

This repository contains the complete workshop documentation for **LinkedIn Essentials**, designed to help college students build a strong professional presence and use LinkedIn strategically to secure internships.

## What This Covers
- Building a standout LinkedIn profile (headline, summary, skills, projects)
- Personal branding for students with little or no experience
- Networking with alumni, recruiters, and professionals
- Using LinkedIn features to increase visibility and opportunities
- Practical templates for outreach, recommendations, and follow-ups

## Who This Is For
- Undergraduate and postgraduate students
- First-time internship seekers
- Students exploring tech, business, research, or design roles

## How to Use This Repo
- Read the documentation sequentially for best results
- Apply changes directly to your LinkedIn profile as you go
- Revisit sections as your skills and goals evolve

## Goal
To help students move from *invisible* to *discoverable* and from *applying blindly* to *applying strategically*.

---

*This workshop is part of a 4-week Internship & Interview Prep Series.*
