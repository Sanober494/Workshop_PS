# Week 3: Interview Mastery

This workshop prepares students to confidently handle all stages of interviews, including behavioral, technical, and final-round interviews, with a strong focus on structured thinking and clear communication.

## What This Covers
- Behavioral interview strategies using the STAR framework
- Technical interview preparation for CS, Data, and ML roles
- Project deep dives and case-style problem solving
- Final-round interview mindset and best practices

## Outcome
By the end of this week, students will be able to:
- Answer behavioral questions with clarity and confidence
- Approach technical questions methodically
- Explain projects in depth and handle follow-up questions
- Navigate final interviews with professionalism and ownership
